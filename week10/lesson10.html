      Validating Forms?

    1. Form validation is something you find on most websites where you either sign up for or create an account for. You will need to provide things such as emails or passwords. Form validation makes sure that the user inputs the right things in the right places. For example, if you are entering your email, you will always have a @ and .com in the email.
